Rotorua

Rotorua is located on New Zealand's North Island. It is one of the most premier tourist destination because of its intense geothermal activity (geysers, mud pools, hot springs), rich Māori culture, and adventure sports

Best Time to Visit: Autumn (March to May) and Spring (September to November)

  • Maori Culture: A central hub for Maori culture, featuring traditional performances, experiences, and historical, cultural sites.
  • Geothermal Attractions: Key spots include bubbling mud pools, natural hot springs, and geysers. Popular, safe areas for viewing include various geothermal parks, with warnings to stay on designated paths.
  • Adventure & Nature: Known for world-class mountain biking in the Redwood forest, hiking, rafting, and ziplining.
